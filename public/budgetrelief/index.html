PROFITstar® Budget™

Provides comprehensive top–down and bottom–up budgeting, as well as forecasting and reporting. It integrates with any core operating system. Whether your budgeting is completed with consolidated financials or at the branch/cost center level and rolled up, this tool gets the budget done quickly and accurately.

You can build multiple what–if models and test various scenarios. Compare the models to determine the most profitable strategic direction. It also offers the ability to generate user–defined scenarios and projections, as well as quickly and easily generate custom variance reports and graphs.

PROFITstar® ALM Budgeting™

Manages risk by closely integrating financial institution initiatives with asset liability management. It tracks and compares "what if" scenarios with built–in strategic monitors that automatically create audit trails. Mid–year reforecasting features allow you stay on top of your budgeting year–round.

You can stress–test key assumptions, conduct interest rate simulations, and perform back testing. It determines the accurate value for FAS107 reporting, and creates detailed, summary, and variance finance and budget reports.

PROFITability® Budget™

Offers a view of the true profitability of a branch, department, or region while delivering comprehensive and flexible budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting. Get a clear, accurate picture of where revenues and expenses are derived.

Among other features, this tool compares allocated expenses as they relate to income, both historically and forecasted. It offers an accurate view of adjusted earnings and return on equity.
