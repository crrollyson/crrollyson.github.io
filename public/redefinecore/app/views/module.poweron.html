Ready to unleash your internal innovation? PowerOn puts your bank in the driver's seat by enabling IT staff to develop tools and automate manual tasks within SilverLake, using standard .NET programming languages. PowerOn gives you the ability to develop interfaces with other systems, and to integrate data from multiple sources so critical information is just a click away. PowerOn can be used to pull data from virtually any source to present a full and complete picture of the account or customer being assisted. In addition, it can be used to supplement or update information stored in the core by performing data look–up and analysis on an account. Changes are made within the structure and protection of the core system, but independent of the core code.
